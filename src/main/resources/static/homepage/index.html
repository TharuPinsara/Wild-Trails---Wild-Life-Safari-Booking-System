About the Experience

WildTrails offers expertly guided wildlife safaris across Sri Lanka's most famous national parks. Whether you're a local nature lover or an overseas explorer, our tours bring you face to face with elephants, leopards, rare birds, and lush jungle landscapes.

Why Choose Us

Certified naturalist guides, comfortable vehicles, and eco-friendly practices ensure a safe and authentic encounter with Sri Lanka's wildlife. Our tours are designed for families, solo travelers, and adventure seekers alike.

  • Yala National Park – World-renowned for leopard sightings.
  • Udawalawe – Home to large herds of majestic elephants.
  • Minneriya – Witness the spectacular 'Gathering' of wild elephants.
  • Wilpattu – Vast lakes and mysterious forest trails.

Choose your safari, pick your date, and reserve online in minutes.

Both Sri Lankan citizens and international guests enjoy secure payments, transparent pricing, and instant confirmation. Whether you live here or are visiting from afar, Sri Lanka's wild heart belongs to everyone. Join a guided journey that celebrates nature while supporting local conservation.
